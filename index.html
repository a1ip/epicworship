Welcome to EpicWorship

EpicWorship is a fully FREE, High-Quality Church projection and presentation software. Also Used by many in live concerts as well.

Beautiful Simplicity

EpicWorship is designed to not bog you down with complex User Interfaces and what-not. You can access all its features very intuitively. If you've ever paid for expensive Church projection software but find out that it was so complex you could only comfortably use 30% of its features, then EpicWorship is for you!

Convention over Configuration

EpicWorship lets you easily and quickly achieve the results you want most of the time without forcing you to configure a whole lot of stuff beforehand, saving you time and yielding a better experience. This isn't just a gimmick with marketing buzz-words, EpicWorship truly lives up to this.

FREE!

...oh and did we forget to say it's totally FREE?

Some Features

  • Quickly and easily enter and Present Songs. Slides are split by a blank line, making for easy copy/paste/presenting of lyrics.
  • Picture or Motion Backgrounds. Motion Video backgrounds support MP4 (H.264-encoded) video files. Other video container formats may work, but are not guaranteed for now.
  • Present any bible scripture in multiple versions. In-built versions include KJV, Amplified, The Message and ESV. Download more from opensong.org
  • Present Video(Mp4, H.264-encoded) and Audio(MP3 or AAC). WMV/WMA/MOV/AVI not supported.
  • Schedule a Countdown Video to begin your services in Style with or without your presence
  • Smooth Text render effects. Your text stands out no matter the background you are putting it on. Choose text color or Text shadow color.
  • Slick slide transition effects. (defaults for now)

System Requirements

  • Windows | Linux | Mac OSX 10.9+ (Mavericks and up)
  • Dual Core processor and up due to sheer visual awesomeness
  • Having a nice video card sure helps too
  • Java 8
  • If you are on Windows XP or Vista, please make sure you have installed video codecs that allow you to view H.264 Mp4 video.
